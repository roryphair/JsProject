How To Play

Welcome to the cybernetic future dear player. Are you ready to finally find out which animal is the hardest? Is it the Chinchilla or the Gibbon?

Assemble your ultimate animal team by clicking on the left sidebar. You can move your animals to any blue square by dragging them. Your animals will fight bravely once you hit Start Fight.

Each wave will require more of your brain power. You can view Animal Stats by mousing over the animal. Fight hard to achieve perfect glory, or cheat with the next wave button.
